About Me

Labelling ourselves. It's a challenge.

~100% of your perception of me will based on the next few lines I spent a fraction of my life to write 😀.

For most of my life I was the one to listen in conversations, absorbing things like a sponge. Why? I have an itch for understanding. Believing there is an explanation for everyhting that can be traced back to a foundational truth.

This path helped me develop visions for a better world. Where rural farmers have larger crop yields. Where 200K patients don't have to die from medical errors. Analyzing the immune system hypothesis for alzheimer's.

Recently, I've also found joy in the unlabeled path. Making music and enjoying stories. Projects with an interesting outcome waiting at the end of a boundless creative process.
