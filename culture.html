Values

Values

Know Thyself. Be Thyself.

Be intentional. Know why you're doing what you're doing. Seek understanding of yourself and what's around you. Be authentic, the people I like the most are authentic because it makes them unique.

Seek Discomfort

I struggle with this the most, but the best experiences come from those exicitng, unconventional situtations. Seeking discomfort ironically makes you a more calm person.

Create what's needed

If you see a hole in your life or the world, don't wait for the problem to be solved. Have agency. Be the change you want to see.

Make Magic

Dream big. Be ambitious. And have a bias towards action. You can make magic.

Change your eye-level

Just when you think you know something, look at it in a different way. Form an opinion, then seek others. Perspective is one of the most valuable things in the world.

Do more with less

You don't need that special thing. You can do a lot more with what you have. Think about how to maximize experiences.
